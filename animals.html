Amazing Animalia

Prepare to be blown out of the water! There are some living creatures that seem so out-of-this-world, it might be difficult for you to believe they exist. These inhabitants of the animal kingdom range from sort of cute to absolutely bonkers. Some you can wrap your head around—others can wrap their heads around you! Think I'm telling tall tails? I get it. You have to see it to believe it!

The amazing animals below are sorted into like kinds. Clicking on the names will (eventually) take you to their wikipedia pages, if they have one.
